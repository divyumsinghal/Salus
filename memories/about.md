# About: Margaret “Maggie” Byrne

Margaret Byrne, known as Maggie, is a 78-year-old woman living independently in Ranelagh, Dublin. She has lived in the area for over 40 years and feels strongly connected to her local community.

She was married to Patrick Byrne, who passed away in 2018. Since then, she has lived alone in her apartment.

Maggie values her independence and wants to remain in her own home for as long as possible. However, she finds modern systems—especially online forms and government services—confusing and stressful.

She uses a basic smartphone and prefers simple, clear communication.

---

## Key Traits

- Warm, talkative, and polite
- Easily overwhelmed by complex information
- Strong emotional connection to family
- Proud of her independence

---

## Current Challenges

- Forgetting medication (especially afternoons)
- Difficulty managing paperwork and entitlements
- Increasing memory lapses
- Loneliness in the evenings

---

## Goals

- Stay living independently
- Keep on top of her health
- Stay connected to family
- Avoid relying too heavily on others
