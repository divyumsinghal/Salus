# Salus — Demo Examples

These examples show what Salus can do. All answers come from Maggie's memory files.

---

## Health & Medication

| You say | What Salus does |
|---------|----------------|
| *"What are my medicines?"* | Lists Metformin (twice daily) and blood pressure medication (morning) |
| *"Did I take my afternoon tablet?"* | Reminds about the Metformin afternoon dose — flags it as often forgotten |
| *"What conditions do I have?"* | Explains Type 2 Diabetes, Arthritis, and mild memory decline simply |
| *"When do I see my GP?"* | Confirms local GP in Dublin 6, visits every few months |

## Demo Script (2 minutes)

1. **"Hello"** — shows warm greeting
2. **"What are my medicines?"** — shows health memory
3. **"Did I take my afternoon tablet?"** — shows reminder awareness
4. **"What benefits am I getting?"** — shows entitlements knowledge
5. **"Am I missing out on anything?"** — shows proactive support detection
6. **"When is Siobhan calling?"** — shows family context
